Choose Your Question Type

With STAAR Test Maker you can choose to present items in the form of

Multiple Choice, Gridded and Fill-In Response, as well as Cognitive Complexity.

Multiple Choice

These items take an average of one minute to answer and have four answer choices labeled as you would see on the real STAAR test: A,B,C,D or F,G,H,I for alternating items. Multiple Choice items have only one correct answer and all distractors are plausible, relevant, and appropriate for the tested grade level.

Cognitive Complexity

STAAR Test Maker follows the Texas Education Agencyâ€™s guidelines when classifying the cognitive complexity of test items. Low, Moderate and High Complexity describe the demands an item may make on a student. We built these classifications into STAAR Test Maker allowing Educators to sort and select test items by cognitive complexity as appropriate for the tested benchmark.

Gridded and Fill-In Response

We make sure masculine pronouns are not used to identify both sexes and these type of items are continuously reviewed to eliminate any indication of bias. Bubble Grids used in Gridded Response test items contain four, five, six, or seven columns with each column containing the digits 0 through 9 enclosed in bubbles. These test items take an average of ninety seconds to answer.
