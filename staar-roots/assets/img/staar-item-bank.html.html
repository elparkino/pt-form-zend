Choose Your Question Type

With STAAR Test Maker you can choose to present items in the form of

Multiple Choice, Gridded and Fill-In Response, as well as Cognitive Complexity.

Multiple Choice

These items take an average of one minute to answer and have four answer choices labeled as you would see on the real STAAR test: A,B,C,D or F,G,H,I for alternating items. Multiple Choice items have only one correct answer and all distractors are plausible, relevant, and appropriate for the tested grade level.

Cognitive Complexity

STAAR Test Maker follows the Texas Education Agency’s guidelines when classifying the cognitive complexity of test items. Low, Moderate and High Complexity describe the demands an item may make on a student. We built these classifications into STAAR Test Maker allowing Educators to sort and select test items by cognitive complexity as appropriate for the tested benchmark.

Gridded and Fill-In Response

We make sure masculine pronouns are not used to identify both sexes and these type of items are continuously reviewed to eliminate any indication of bias. Bubble Grids used in Gridded Response test items contain four, five, six, or seven columns with each column containing the digits 0 through 9 enclosed in bubbles. These test items take an average of ninety seconds to answer.

Writing

All prompts adhere to Composition, Revision, and Editing Reporting Categories and are phrased and formatted to match the STAAR Readiness and Supporting Standards.

Science

STAAR Test Maker’s Science item bank is the only item bank written specifically for Texas schools including thousands of items that test for their appropriate Reporting Categories such as Matter and Energy, Earth and Space, Organisms and Environments, and Force, Motion, and Energy. All Science items adhere to current science methodologies and to the Intel International Science and Engineering Fair Rules and Guidelines as well as match the latest TEKS and STAAR Readiness and Supporting Standards.

Beautiful Design: All graphics are custom designed for STAAR Test Maker Science items by our in-house item writers, editors and graphic artists.
